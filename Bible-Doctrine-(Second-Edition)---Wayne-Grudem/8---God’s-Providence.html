God, in preserving all things he has made, also causes them to maintain the properties with which he created them. This providence, therefore, provides a basis for science. God has made and continues to sustain a universe that acts in predictable ways, so if a scientific experiment gives a certain result today, then we can have confidence that (if all the factors are the same) it will give the same result tomorrow and a hundred years from tomorrow.
