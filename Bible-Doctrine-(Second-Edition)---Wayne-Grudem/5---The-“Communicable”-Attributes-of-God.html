Such a God would not be worthy of our worship, for sin is hateful and it is worthy of being hated. Sin ought not to be. It is in fact a virtue to hate evil and sin (cf. Zech 8:17; Heb 1:9; etc.). This wrath is the response of a loving God towards that which seeks to destroy love - sin.
